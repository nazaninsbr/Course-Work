How to prepare your CV:
	
	1. make an impression in 30 seconds 
	2. highlight all key information 
	3. present yourself as a problem solver 
	4. research a company 
	5. prepare a differenc CV for different job types 

	- dont include irrelevant personal info (dont add your age or marital status on the cover letter)
	- dont lie on your cv 
	- include responsibilities and accomplishments 
	- include accomplishments that are applicable to the job requirement 
	- include hard skills not just soft skills 
	- include experiences that add value 

	Format: 
	+ 1- 2 pages 
	+ month and year
	+ typos, punctuation and grammar
